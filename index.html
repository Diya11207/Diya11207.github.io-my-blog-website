About Chennai

Chennai is a vibrant coastal city located in Tamil Nadu, India. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, classical music, beautiful beaches, and delicious South Indian food.

From the calm vibes of Marina Beach to the architectural beauty of temples, Chennai offers a perfect mix of tradition and modern life.
